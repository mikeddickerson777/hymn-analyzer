1689 Hymn Analyzer

Gemini Live Cognitive Analysis Engine

This was created as a free resource to support Pastors and Worship Pastors as they assess possible songs for worship. The analysis is done by Gemini AI (3.5 Flash) and compares the songs to the 1689 2nd London Baptist Confession for doctrinal accuracy. It uses this scale for scoring: Weights: 70% Doctrine, 20% Narrative Density, and 10% Corporate Singability.

If you encounter any issues or have questions, please contact me at mikeddickerson@gmail.com.

⚠️

Please search the existing database before entering a song under "Analyze Songs", as putting in an existing song will create a duplicate record.
